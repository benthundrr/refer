Program Terms & Fine Print
  • Referrers earn 1% of their referred creator's settlement GMV for a full year (12 months) from the date the creator links with Thundrr.
  • Payments are made after settlement GMV is calculated and finalized, and are processed on a monthly basis.
  • All payments are sent to the PayPal email provided at sign-up — please keep it accurate and active.
  • If the referred creator unlinks from Thundrr, referral payments stop.
  • The 1% applies to settlement GMV only (net of refunds, returns, cancellations and platform adjustments) — not gross sales.
  • The referred creator must remain actively linked and in good standing with TikTok Shop and Thundrr for payments to accrue.
  • Self-referrals and duplicate or fraudulent sign-ups are not eligible and may be voided.
  • Thundrr may amend or end the program at any time; GMV already accrued will be honored.
